This guide walks you through everything you need to go from a brand-new visitor to an active learner on LearnFlow. By the end you will have a verified account, an active session, and a clear view of how to find and enrol in your first course.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Navigate to the [Registration page](/auth/registration) and fill in your details to create your LearnFlow account.

    Provide your email address and choose a strong password that meets the following requirements:

    - At least **8 characters** long
    - Contains at least one **uppercase letter** (A–Z)
    - Contains at least one **digit** (0–9)
    - Contains at least one **special character** (e.g. `!`, `@`, `#`, `$`)

    <Tip>
      Pick a password you have not used elsewhere. LearnFlow stores your password as a secure hash and never exposes it, but using a unique password adds an extra layer of protection for your account.
    </Tip>

    Once you submit the form, LearnFlow creates your account and immediately sends a verification email to the address you provided.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Open your inbox and look for an email from LearnFlow with the subject **"Verify your email address"**. The verification link inside is valid for **24 hours**.

    Click the link to activate your account. You will be redirected to LearnFlow and shown a confirmation that your email address has been verified.

    <Note>
      Didn't receive the email? Check your spam or junk folder first. If it is not there, use the **Resend Verification Email** link on the login page to request a fresh link.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In">
    Go to the [Login page](/auth/login), enter your verified email address and password, then click **Sign In**.

    On success, LearnFlow issues you a JWT access token and redirects you to your personalised learner dashboard. Your session lasts **15 minutes** and refreshes silently in the background as long as you remain active — so you will not be interrupted mid-course.

    <Info>
      If you enter an incorrect password multiple times, LearnFlow will temporarily lock your account to protect it against brute-force attacks. Wait 15 minutes, then try again or use the password reset flow.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore Your Dashboard">
    Your dashboard is your learning home base. From here you can:

    - **Browse available courses** — search and filter the full course catalogue to find topics that interest you.
    - **Track your enrolments** — see every course you have enrolled in, along with its current status.
    - **View your learning progress** — monitor completion percentages and pick up exactly where you left off.

    Select any course card to open its detail page, then click **Enrol** to add it to your learning plan and begin your first lesson.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log Out and Manage Your Account">
    When you finish a session, click **Log Out** from the navigation menu. LearnFlow immediately invalidates your session tokens server-side, so your account stays safe even on shared or public devices.

    If you ever forget your password, use the **Forgot password?** link on the login page to request a reset email. Enter your registered address and LearnFlow will send you a secure, one-time reset link valid for **1 hour**. Click the link, set your new password, and log in fresh — all existing sessions are automatically ended for your security.

    <Tip>
      After resetting your password you will need to log in again on all of your devices. This is intentional — it ensures any unauthorised session is immediately closed.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
