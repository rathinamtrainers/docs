LearnFlow takes your account security seriously. This page covers the protections built into the platform and what you can do to keep your account safe.

## Brute-force protection

LearnFlow automatically defends your account against unauthorised access attempts.

- After **5 failed login attempts within 10 minutes**, your account is automatically locked for **15 minutes**.
- A **warning email is sent to your registered address** as soon as a lockout occurs, so you're always informed.
- You don't have to wait out the full lockout window — you can **unlock your account early by resetting your password**.

<Tip>
  If you receive a lockout alert and you didn't attempt to log in, change your
  password immediately. Someone else may be trying to access your account.
</Tip>

## Secure credential storage

Your password is protected from the moment you create it.

- Your password is **never stored in plain text** — LearnFlow uses industry-standard hashing algorithms to protect it at rest.
- LearnFlow **never logs or exposes your password** in any system, audit trail, or support tool.

## Session security

LearnFlow keeps your active session secure throughout your time on the platform.

- Sessions use **short-lived access tokens** that expire automatically, limiting the window of exposure if a token is ever intercepted.
- If LearnFlow detects a potential session compromise — such as a refresh token being reused unexpectedly — it **immediately signs out all your active sessions across every device** and sends you a security alert email.
- **Resetting your password instantly invalidates all active sessions** on all your devices, ensuring that anyone who may have had unauthorised access is locked out right away.

## Privacy compliance

LearnFlow is built with your privacy rights in mind.

- The platform is designed to meet **DPDPA 2023** and **GDPR-aligned** data protection standards.
- Your personal information is **pseudonymised in system logs** — your email address and other identifiers are never exposed in internal audit trails.
- You have the right to request **access to or deletion of your personal data** at any time. Contact our support team to make such a request.

## Best practices for you

You play an important role in keeping your account secure. Follow these steps to stay protected:

- Use a **strong, unique password** for your LearnFlow account — don't reuse passwords from other services.
- **Never share your password or an active session** with anyone, including colleagues or support staff.
- **Log out when using shared or public devices** to prevent others from accessing your account.
- **Act promptly if you receive an unexpected security alert** — change your password immediately and contact support if you're unsure.
