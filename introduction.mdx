## What you can do with LearnFlow

LearnFlow is designed around learner independence and security. Here is what you can do from the moment your account is active:

- <Icon icon="circle-check" /> **Self-register and verify your email address** — create your account in seconds and confirm your identity through a time-limited verification link.
- <Icon icon="lock" /> **Log in securely with JWT-based authentication** — every session is protected by short-lived access tokens that refresh automatically while you are active.
- <Icon icon="graduation-cap" /> **Enrol in courses and track your learning progress** — browse the course catalogue, enrol with one click, and monitor your progress from your dashboard.
- <Icon icon="key" /> **Reset your password independently** — regain access to your account through a self-service password reset flow, no support ticket required.
- <Icon icon="shield-halved" /> **Stay protected with automatic brute-force lockout** — LearnFlow detects repeated failed login attempts and temporarily locks the account to keep it safe.
