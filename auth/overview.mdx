LearnFlow uses email-and-password authentication to keep your account secure. After you log in, LearnFlow receives a short-lived access token that is automatically managed by the platform — you never need to handle tokens manually. Your session refreshes silently in the background while you're active, so you can focus on learning without interruption.

## Authentication flows

LearnFlow provides four authentication flows that cover every stage of your account lifecycle:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Registration & Email Verification">
    Create your account with an email address and password. LearnFlow sends a verification email to confirm your identity before your account is activated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Login">
    Sign in with your verified email and password to access your dashboard, enrolled courses, and learning progress.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Session Management">
    LearnFlow keeps you logged in automatically. Your session refreshes silently while you're active, and expires safely after a period of inactivity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Password Reset">
    Forgot your password? Reset it yourself using your registered email address — no need to contact support.
  </Step>
</Steps>
