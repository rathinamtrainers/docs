LearnFlow keeps you logged in automatically using a secure session so you can focus on learning without constant interruptions. This page explains what a session is, how long it lasts, and how to end it safely when you're done.

## How your session works

- When you log in, LearnFlow creates a secure session tied to your account.
- Your session is **automatically renewed** while you're actively using the platform — there's no need to log in again mid-course.
- If you're **inactive for too long**, LearnFlow will prompt you to log in again to keep your account secure.
- Your credentials are never stored in your browser beyond the login page — LearnFlow handles all session management securely on its servers.

## Logging out

Always log out when you've finished using LearnFlow — especially on a shared or public device.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your profile menu">
    Click your **profile icon** in the top-right corner of any LearnFlow page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Log Out">
    Click **Log Out** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your session ends immediately">
    LearnFlow invalidates your session on its servers straight away. Even if someone had previously copied your session details, they become unusable the moment you log out.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Session invalidation

<Info>
  Certain actions automatically end all your active sessions across every device:

  - **Resetting your password** immediately signs out all sessions on all devices. Log in again with your new password after the reset is complete.
  - **Unusual activity detected** — if LearnFlow detects a potential security issue (such as a session being reused in an unexpected way), it will sign out all your devices and send you a security alert email to let you know.
</Info>

## If your session expires unexpectedly

<Accordion title="Why did my session end early?">
  There are a few reasons your session might end sooner than expected:

  **Possible causes:**
  - You were inactive for an extended period and the session timed out naturally.
  - You (or someone with access to your account) reset your password on another device — this ends all active sessions immediately.
  - LearnFlow's security system detected unusual session activity and signed out all devices as a precaution.

  **What to do:**
  - Return to the [login page](/auth/login) and sign in again with your email and password.
  - If your session keeps ending unexpectedly and you haven't reset your password or been inactive, [contact LearnFlow support](mailto:support@learnflow.com) — it may indicate unusual activity on your account.
</Accordion>
