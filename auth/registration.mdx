Getting started with LearnFlow takes just a few moments. Provide your email address and choose a password, and LearnFlow will send you a verification email to confirm your identity. Once you click the link in that email, your account is active and you're ready to enrol in courses.

## Password requirements

Choose a password that meets all of the following rules:

- At least **8 characters** long
- At least **one uppercase letter** (A–Z)
- At least **one digit** (0–9)
- At least **one special character** (e.g. `!@#$%^&*`)
- Must **not** be the same as your email address
- Must **not** be a commonly used or widely known password

## What happens after you register

<Steps>
  <Step title="Account created — pending verification">
    LearnFlow creates your account with a "Pending Verification" status. You cannot log in until your email address is verified.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verification email sent">
    LearnFlow sends a verification email to the address you provided. The link inside is valid for **24 hours**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the link in the email">
    Open the email and click the verification link. This confirms that the email address belongs to you and activates your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your account is now active">
    You're redirected to the login page with a confirmation message. Your account is active — go ahead and log in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Didn't receive the verification email?** Check your spam or junk folder first. If it's not there, use the **Resend verification email** link on the login page to get a fresh one.
</Note>

<Info>
  Already have an account? Head to [Login](/auth/login) to sign in.
</Info>

## Common registration errors

| Error | What it means | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| **Email already registered** (409) | An active account exists with this email address. | Go to the [login page](/auth/login) to sign in instead. |
| **Email registered but not verified** (409) | You registered before but never verified your email. | Use the **Resend verification email** link on the login page. |
| **Password too weak** (422) | Your password doesn't meet one or more complexity rules. | Review the [password requirements](#password-requirements) above and try again. |
| **Invalid email format** (422) | The email address you entered isn't formatted correctly. | Double-check your email and re-enter it (e.g. `name@example.com`). |
